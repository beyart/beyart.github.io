Fruit Growing in Tropical/Sub-tropical And Temperate Regions

Agriculture is a backborne of a country especially for developing countries. Oranges are among the fruits rich in vitamin C. They are also income earners to a country.

Budded oranges are healthier and help reduce the maturity period. Oranges which could take ten years to start bearing fruits when budded/grafted now start bearing at maximum period of three to four years.

It is highly recommended to bud your seedlings to boost productivity. Grafted oranges have high resistance to orange diseases.
